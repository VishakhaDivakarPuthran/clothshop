About Vishakha

Meet Vishakha

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Vishakha, the proud owner of this cloth shop in Boloor, Mangalore. This journey started as a hobby project when I decided to build a mobile application to bring our vibrant clothing collection closer to the community.

Being passionate about technology and fashion, I combined my skills to create a platform that not only showcases quality clothing but also provides an easy and affordable way for people in my hometown to shop from trusted brands.

Our Vision

At Vishakha’s Cloth Shop, our mission is to make stylish, high-quality clothing accessible to everyone in Boloor at reasonable prices. With this application, I aim to bridge the gap between traditional retail and modern technology, offering a seamless shopping experience.

We handpick our collection from trusted brands, ensuring that every item meets our quality and affordability standards. Whether you're looking for casual wear, formal attire, or trendy pieces, we’ve got something for everyone.
